Appetizers: The Perfect Introduction to Your Menu

The appetizer section sets the tone for your entire menu. Choose dishes that are light yet flavorful to stimulate the appetite without overwhelming your guests. Include a mix of textures and flavors—crispy, creamy, tangy, or savory—to appeal to a variety of tastes. Don’t forget to consider portion sizes; appetizers should be small enough to leave room for the main courses but satisfying enough to impress on their own.

Main Courses: The Heart of Your Menu

Main courses are the centerpiece of your menu. Choose dishes that are hearty, satisfying, and showcase the culinary identity of your restaurant. Offer a mix of proteins, vegetarian options, and signature specials. Keep portions generous but balanced, and ensure every dish pairs well with your appetizers and sides to create a cohesive dining experience.
